Salmon Flow!

Using the controls, avoid obstacles like rocks, logs, fisherman, beavers, bears, and birds. Jump over beaver dams or else face instant level restart! Eat flies or worms to gain health! If you run out of health, it is game over!
Rocks and logs = -5 health | Beavers and Birds = -10 health | Bears and Fisherman = -15 health | Flies = +6 health | Worms = +9 health |
Beaver Dams = Level Restart | 0 Health = Game Over!

Left arrow to move left | Right arrow to move right | Space Bar to jump
