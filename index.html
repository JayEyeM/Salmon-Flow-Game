Game Instructions & Controls

Avoid obstacles like driftwood and beavers, or else game over! Use your jumps wisely! You get three jumps to bypass obstacles. It takes 15 seconds for all three to renew!
The salmon continues to drift in the last pressed direction, so be careful and keep swimming!
If you're quick enough, you can score extra points by eating flies!
Flies = +5 points
